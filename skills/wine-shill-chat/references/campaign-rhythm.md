# Campaign Rhythm

Use this when the task has preheat, sale, return, afternoon, next-day, multi-wave, or "previous wave already sold" context.

## Stage Modes

| Stage | Main job | Good signals |
| --- | --- | --- |
| preheat | Build recognition before link or price | official account explains origin, group asks what it is, someone waits for price |
| sale | Convert interest into orders | link appears, price is clarified, orders and screenshots start |
| afternoon_reorder | Follow up on missed or hesitant buyers | "刚才拍了", "上次没补到", "朋友也要", second order |
| return_close | Use return-event context without shouting | previous sell-out, current favorable price, a few remaining hesitations |
| arrival_feedback | Let proof replace claims | delivery, packaging, open-bottle, dinner, family feedback |
| collection_close | Close an event or product list | use-case grouping, missed-reader guidance, final registration |

Do not force a stage name into output unless the user asks for labeled sections.

## Explicit Group Variant Rhythm

Only apply this when the user explicitly says the group is old, new, next-new, or mixed. Do not infer the variant silently.

- Old group: selling rhythm starts from familiarity. Use prior drinking, repurchase, missed restock, family feedback, "上次链接没了", "上次买少了", and补货. Price-control or return context should enter as memory or a current link/price incident, not as a slogan.
- New or next-new group: selling rhythm starts from risk reduction. Use "啥来头", "值不值", "跟K6/熟悉酒比怎么样", "喝过的人说一句", price doubt, and small service answers. Let people convert after answers, not immediately.
- Mixed group: old members should answer new members unevenly. A few people know prior waves; others ask basic value or taste questions.

## Official vs Group Split

- `官号` can speak more completely than ordinary members: product identity, campaign time, link, confirmed price, gifts, and official activity facts.
- When the user asks for官号内容, official sales content, or the existing HLY official-account format, `官号` is not a short notice. Use a three-paragraph + three-image structure. Each paragraph should sell a different angle with literary, image-rich language.
- In that format, paragraph 1 raises brand/product recognition, paragraph 2 turns story, craft, awards, or lineage into trust, and paragraph 3 continues the sales narrative through value, taste, dignity, gift feel, or daily-drinking logic. The third paragraph may lightly carry price, rules, or countdown at the end, but it must remain a selling paragraph.
- On old-group repeat campaigns, the group-history hook can appear inside the literary structure, but do not compress `官号` into 3-5 dry notice lines when the user expects three image-backed paragraphs.
- Ordinary members must transform official facts into lived reactions: price doubt, "上次没补到", taste question, order screenshot, gift registration, dinner scene, or competitor comparison.
- Do not let ordinary members simply rephrase the official announcement.

## 官号 + 云中客 Packages

Use this shape when the user asks for both official copy and shill chat.

- For HLY-style官号内容, default `官号` to three paragraphs with three specific image placeholders:
  - Paragraph 1: brand/product opening with a strong sentence, not a bland description.
  - `【图1：产品主图/品牌主视觉】`
  - Paragraph 2: story, award, craft, lineage, or taste proof made literary and persuasive.
  - `【图2：品牌背书/工艺/瓶身细节/奖项图】`
  - Paragraph 3: value and use-worth, still as selling copy; add price/rule/countdown only in the last one or two short lines if provided.
  - `【图3：活动图/价格图/倒计时图】`
- `官号` paragraphs can be longer than one chat line, especially for holiday, full-day, collection, or官号内容. Keep them copyable and mobile-readable, but let them carry romance, authority, and desire. Avoid product-manual sprawl.
- `云中客` should be true group chat: default to about 30 lines per segment unless the user asks for a different length.
- Do not make every line advance the sale. A believable `云中客` section can include light waste talk, jokes, a side question, someone hesitating, someone talking about family, and then naturally circling back to the wine.
- If the user says "云中客全部长一点", expand the chat by adding new social events, not by stretching the same praise.
- In old/new group pairs, do not reuse the same opening, buyer, and expert order. The old group may say "上次买少了"; the next-new group should ask "这酒啥来头" or "跟熟悉款比怎么样".
- `客服` appears for registration, link, gift, and rule facts. `官号` can make campaign announcements. Neither should fake personal drinking experience.

### 官号内容 Voice

Use this voice when the user asks for官号内容,文艺宣传,卖点故事,奖项故事, or says the official content should resemble the Feishu/HLY style.

- Write like a private-domain wine seller with taste, not like客服. Lead with a sentence that creates status or memory: `酒友都知道五粮液是酒界公认的浓香天花板` or `泸州这座城，最出名的不是山水，是那一口浓香`.
- Convert facts into story: big-factory lineage, benchmark aroma style, historic award, old cellar, non-heritage craft, master blending, banquet memory, or "ordinary drinkers can finally open it" value.
- Use phrases such as `大厂降维打击`, `硬核基因`, `品质底线`, `普通酒友也舍得开`, `不是陌生概念`, `把大厂浓香做成日常饭桌也能打开的样子` only when the facts support them.
- Third paragraph must not become logistics. Do not write empty lines like `这款适合的场景很清楚`, `价格规则看当前页面`, `按页面为准`, or `拍完截图登记` as the main content. If activity facts are needed, place them as a light tail after the value story.
- For old group, weave prior sale memory into the romance: `上次喝过的人知道，它不是靠热闹撑起来的`; for next-new group, explain why the wine is worth first trying through brand lineage and risk reduction.

Good official paragraph pattern:

```text

从老窖池里走出来的窖香、粮香、曲香，养出了中国浓香白酒最鲜明的底色。
泸州鉴藏喝的不是陌生概念，而是泸州老窖一脉相承的浓香底子：开瓶有香，入口有劲，落喉有余味。
【图1：产品主图】

真正的大厂酒，贵在有底线。
不是靠包装讲故事，而是靠窖池、技艺、酒体，把一瓶浓香该有的骨架撑起来。
泸州老窖有“浓香鼻祖”的名声，有国宝窖池和非遗酿造技艺的底蕴，泸州鉴藏承接的，就是这种老牌浓香的稳、正、厚。
【图2：品牌/窖池/瓶身细节图】

这支酒最妙的地方，是把大厂浓香做成了普通酒友也舍得开的样子。
透明礼盒有体面，52度浓香有酒劲，酒体不飘，饭桌上能立住，送出去也不轻。

【图3：活动图/价格图】
```

## HLY-Style Timing Patterns

Use the user's requested schedule first. If they ask for a familiar private-domain flow without exact times, these patterns are proven:

- Two-day single product: 前一天预热 -> 当天中午开卖 -> 当天晚上提醒 -> 第二天中午证据/奖项/反馈 -> 第二天晚上收口.
- One-day holiday product: 前一天预热 -> 当天中午主卖 -> 当天晚上父亲/端午/家宴场景收口.
- Full-day event: 前一天8点预热 -> 当天9点总预热 -> hourly product waves -> 22点合集收口.
- Collection close: `官号` writes a warm guide by场景 and need; `云中客` shows different people filling missed orders, comparing use cases, and reminding screenshots.

## Old vs Next-New Angle Split

When both versions are requested, intentionally separate the daily focus:

- Old group: fewer basic facts; more prior sale, good feedback,回购,涨价/控价 memory, missed restock, family feedback, and "这次为什么补".
- Next-new group: more brand, origin,工艺, awards if provided, "为什么值得试", competitor anchor, and risk reduction before orders.
- If the user provides daily focus, follow it exactly. Otherwise do not repeat the same angle on both versions on the same day.

## Product-Pattern Notes

Use these as pattern references, not as fixed scripts.

- Repeat sauce-aroma return, old group: anchor the official copy on "上次卖过/中间少量补过/这次协调到一波/价格或活动有变化". Leave some new shill roles to ask price, taste, and stock questions; do not let the same old buyers carry every beat.
- Strong old-group closeout, gift or holiday stock-up: anchor on "最后几小时", "昨天到今天有人按节日用酒拍", "群里喝过的人多", and "喝完愿意回头补". This is stronger than a long explanation of grain, brewing, or awards.
- If a rich product story is useful, split it into mobile-length official lines or move it into one natural group question. Long encyclopedia paragraphs are draft material, not final HLY output.

## Shill State Ledger

Track a small internal state for ordinary roles across waves:

```yaml
shill_states:
  bought: []
  watching: []
  waiting_for_price: []
  waiting_for_taste: []
  waiting_for_delivery: []
  reordering: []
  reserved_for_later: []
```

Rules:

- In multi-wave scripts, leave a few ordinary roles unconverted for later. Do not make every shill buy in the first wave.
- In repeat-campaign scripts, explicitly reserve a few fresh questioners for later waves so the group does not look like the same few people are always performing.
- "Reserved for later" roles can become buyers in a later wave because of price, taste proof, delivery, gift upgrade, or another member's screenshot.
- A buyer can reorder later, but give a reason: gift calculation, friend asks for one, family stock, family feedback, holiday meal, missed previous restock, price confirmation, or delivery/taste proof.
- Preserve believable non-buyers. Some people may only ask, doubt, or say they will wait for delivery.

## What To Avoid

- Do not convert all named roles immediately.
- Do not let `官号` and ordinary members say the same selling points back-to-back.
- Do not make every section open with "我又补了一箱"; vary with family feedback, missed restock, expert challenge, price doubt, or photo reaction.
- Do not turn collection close into a full product catalog unless links are explicitly requested.
- Do not keep long technical paragraphs merely because they are factually correct. If the final use is WeChat/private-domain, short copyable lines beat complete background explanation.
- Do not write hidden production notes such as "找个新托", "留这里发", "这一段写不出来".
- Do not make gift-upgrade or same-day-combine claims unless the user supplied them.
