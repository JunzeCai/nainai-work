# Quality Gate

Check before final output:

- Facts in the chat are in `fact_lock`.
- Customer service is named `客服`, `官方客服`, or `官号` and does not fake personal drinking experience.
- `官号` may announce confirmed product/campaign facts, but ordinary members must not simply repeat the announcement.
- About 30 lines per `云中客` segment unless user specified otherwise.
- At least 6 ordinary roles when the segment is full length.
- First 5 lines contain a hook: bought, arrived, price, gift, comparison, or question.
- At least one doubt and one correction. Corrections must sound like real group talk, such as `别吹这么满`, `我看没那么玄`, or `先看入口冲不冲`; reject teacher-like summary lines such as `定位放准就舒服` or `反馈比空夸有用`.
- Image placeholders are specific and have before/after chat.
- No internal notes or screenshot numbers.
- No generic host/script language.
- Different segments introduce new angles instead of repeating the same卖点.
- User-provided selling points are allowed; reject only invented facts or stiff slogan delivery.
- Each major selling point enters through a social move: question, doubt, correction, personal reason, customer-service fact, or life scene.
- No ordinary member stacks two or more selling points in one stiff line unless it is framed as a natural question or correction.
- Multi-version output follows a single 3-day / 6-wave timeline when requested or implied.
- Each wave has a distinct main focus: taste, questions, order/arrival, awards/reputation, life scene, natural wrap-up.
- Ordinary members do not repeat detail-page parameters such as "52度浓香500毫升", "净含量500ml", or type/spec strings as praise; if provided as selling points, specs appear sparingly inside natural questions or corrections.
- No ordinary member says or implies process questions such as "还能不能拍", "客服能不能拍", "问客服还能拍吗", or similar.
- Process questions are replaced by actions such as 已拍, 补单, 截图登记, 等到货, 晚上开了说, or 周末试.
- No ordinary member uses deadline pressure such as "最后一天抓紧", "不拍就没了", "今晚必须下手", or similar; provided deadlines may appear only as calm factual context.
- Provided official price-control notes such as "官方控价799" may appear, but not as a stiff slogan, not as leaked inside information, and not combined with pressure language.
- Awards/honors appear only if provided and are mentioned casually by at most one or two roles in a wave.
- Abstract positioning words such as "知名度", "神秘度", "权威感", "更柔", and "更深" are not repeated across roles or waves.
- Taste claims include lived context when possible, such as meal, family, throat feel, aftertaste, or next drinking occasion.
- Role rotation is believable: the same ordinary member is not repeatedly the buyer, photo poster, expert, and closer without a callback reason.
- Multi-wave scripts keep some ordinary roles unconverted for later unless the user explicitly asks that everyone has bought.
- Later-wave conversions or reorders have a reason: price confirmation, taste proof, delivery proof, gift calculation, family stock, friend asks for one, or missed previous restock.
- Orders and reorders have visible human reasons: prior taste, family feedback, holiday meal, gift calculation, missed restock, price confirmation, or delivery/taste proof.
- When old/new/mixed group is explicitly requested, rhythm matches the variant: old group uses history and repurchase; new or next-new group uses questions, risk reduction, and value proof; mixed group has uneven knowledge.
- Familiar-wine comparisons, such as K6, are personal and concrete, not synchronized praise; include a caveat when natural.
- Persona behavior matches product tier, and similar-looking names are not clustered in a way that makes the chat read like one person.
- Stage rhythm is coherent when specified: preheat builds recognition, sale clarifies price/link and starts orders, afternoon/reorder handles second orders and missed buyers, arrival feedback uses proof.
- Campaign-package sections follow the requested format: if the user asks for 官号+云中客, both are present; if the user asks for old/new or next-new groups, each version has a distinct angle and knowledge level.
- If the user asks for官号内容, Feishu/HLY official format, or image-backed official copy, each `官号` section has exactly three selling paragraphs and three specific image placeholders.
- In three-paragraph official account content, paragraph 3 remains卖点宣传. It may mention price/rules/countdown only lightly at the end; it must not become a logistics notice.
- `官号` copy is copyable and compact, but it should be literary and persuasive when requested: brand story, awards, craft, lineage, taste, and value should replace empty service phrasing.
- Reject meaningless official lines such as `这款适合的场景很清楚`, `价格规则看当前页面`, `按页面为准`, or `拍完截图登记` when they carry the main paragraph.
- Old-group repeat or closeout `官号` may include prior sale/补货 and group proof, but should not lose the approved literary selling voice when the user wants official account content.
- `云中客` sections add social events and role movement, not repeated praise. Longer sections add new reasons, questions, photo reactions, or callbacks.
- `云中客` sounds口语化 and middle-aged: not official, not host-like, not a series of neat summary sentences. Reject lines such as `反馈比空夸有用`, `品牌好比讲参数有用`, or `定位放准就舒服` unless rewritten into lived family/meal/budget/taste speech.
- `云中客` is not a sales-progress checklist. Reject sections where almost every line pushes price, rule, order,客服, or conversion; keep a few side-chat, family, joke, hesitation, or callback lines that make the group feel alive.
- Repeat-campaign role rotation reserves fresh questioners instead of reusing the same few shill names in every wave.
- Collection close groups products by use case or need when helpful and does not write every product link unless requested.
- No hidden production notes appear, including "找个新托", "留四五个托", "留这里发", "这一段写不出来", or similar.
- Old/new/mixed group handling appears only when the user explicitly requested that structure.
- If the request says 600 total entries, the six-wave allocation totals 600; if it says 200 per six waves, the plan recognizes that total as 1200.

Rewrite once if any hard gate fails.
