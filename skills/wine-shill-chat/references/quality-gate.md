# Quality Gate

Check before final output:

- Facts in the chat are in `fact_lock`.
- Customer service is named `客服`, `官方客服`, or `官号` and does not fake personal drinking experience.
- 25-30 lines per segment unless user specified otherwise.
- At least 6 ordinary roles when the segment is full length.
- First 5 lines contain a hook: bought, arrived, price, gift, comparison, or question.
- At least one doubt and one correction.
- Image placeholders are specific and have before/after chat.
- No internal notes or screenshot numbers.
- No generic host/script language.
- Different segments introduce new angles instead of repeating the same卖点.
- Multi-version output follows a single 3-day / 6-wave timeline when requested or implied.
- Each wave has a distinct main focus: taste, questions, order/arrival, awards/reputation, life scene, natural wrap-up.
- Ordinary members do not repeat detail-page parameters such as "52度浓香500毫升", "净含量500ml", or type/spec strings as praise.
- No ordinary member says or implies process questions such as "还能不能拍", "客服能不能拍", "问客服还能拍吗", or similar.
- No ordinary member uses deadline pressure such as "最后一天抓紧", "不拍就没了", "今晚必须下手", or similar.
- No one exposes internal/official price-control notes such as "官方控价799很严", "控价很死", or hidden channel rules.
- Awards/honors appear only if provided and are mentioned casually by at most one or two roles in a wave.
- Abstract positioning words such as "知名度", "神秘度", "权威感", "更柔", and "更深" are not repeated across roles or waves.
- Role rotation is believable: the same ordinary member is not repeatedly the buyer, photo poster, expert, and closer without a callback reason.
- If the request says 600 total entries, the six-wave allocation totals 600; if it says 200 per six waves, the plan recognizes that total as 1200.

Rewrite once if any hard gate fails.
