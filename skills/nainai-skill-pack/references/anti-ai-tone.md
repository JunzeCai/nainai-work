# Anti-AI Tone

The output should feel like a person writing in a private WeChat context, not a model completing a content brief.

## Hard Bans

- Do not write task scaffolding: "以下是", "开场", "中段", "总结", "我来分析".
- Do not use brochure phrases: "匠心传承", "彰显尊贵", "品质与性价比兼具".
- Do not overuse universal hype: "闭眼冲", "性价比拉满", "这波很稳", "方向是对的".
- Do not make every line carry a full selling point.
- Do not make all roles speak in the same sentence length or expert voice.
- Do not turn chat into a news summary, product manual, tasting note, or host script.

## Repair Rules

- Break stiff complete sentences into questions and half-sentences, but do not make every line short. For `云中客`, especially middle-aged wine-friend chats, mix short reactions with a few looser, slightly wordy family/meal/budget lines so the group does not read like clipped official summaries.
- Add interruption, correction, and small misunderstandings.
- Hide facts inside question-answer exchanges instead of listing them.
- Let some lines be pure reaction: "啊？", "别急", "真有这事？", "那倒也是".
- Replace polished adjectives with lived context: dinner, family, express delivery, gift, budget, old father, weekend gathering.

## Quality Smell

If deleting the names before each line still leaves a smooth essay, rewrite. Real group chat needs uneven rhythm, incomplete information, and role friction.
