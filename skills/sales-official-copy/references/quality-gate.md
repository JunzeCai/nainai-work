# Quality Gate

Check before final output:

- Every price, coupon, gift, stock, deadline, award, official claim, origin, year, batch, and link is in the fact lock.
- Links are unchanged.
- One version only.
- No title or explanation.
- The opening reaches the product within 2-4 lines.
- Paragraphs are short and copyable.
- Tone is private-domain, not brochure, live-stream yelling, or AI summary.
- If facts are missing, the copy is shorter instead of padded.

Hard fail examples:

- "库存最后300箱" without user/source support.
- "官价XXX" without user/source support.
- Rewriting or shortening a URL.
- Writing "爆卖" without sales evidence.
